What is our goal?

The goal of VIPR is to expedite the weekly tasks of filling out vehicle inspection reports and mechanic repairs. VIPR will provide mechanics with enough information to make the best decisions and preplan repairs on vehicles. It will also keep drivers updated on repairs, and allow drivers to see a more detailed explanation of what goes into repairs.
